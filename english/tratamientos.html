How is the first date?

Medical history:
  • At the beginning of the consultation, the dentist is responsible for filling out the file with the patient's medical history and evaluating the main reason for the visit. This information will allow you to continue with the periodontal examination looking directly for the causes of the problems that afflict the patient
  • It is very important that the patient provides all the relevant information about their health because some problems may arise due to ignorance of some special medical conditions such as allergies, cardiovascular diseases, diabetes and infectious diseases. In the case of women, it is essential to inform if there is pregnancy
  • The patient should not hide clinical information for fear that it will be disseminated. The data provided is strictly confidential and are protected based on the Federal Law Personal Data Protection.
Diagnóstico y Tratamiento
  • After having filled the clinic story and effected the valoration with the intra-orally camera, the dentist will make a personalized diagnosis to the patient and will propose the most appropriate treatment to resolve the problem detected
  • Once the dentist will resolve all the patient doubts, the first consultation will be completed
